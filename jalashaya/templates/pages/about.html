Founded 2018

A Journey of
Pure Purpose

Jalashaya (meaning "abode of water" in Sanskrit) was founded with one powerful belief — every Indian deserves access to pure, mineral-rich drinking water without compromise.

Our founders, after witnessing water quality issues in urban India, decided to build a company that sources from pristine Himalayan springs and delivers purity at scale.

Today, we serve over 50,000 families across 25 cities, with a team of 200+ dedicated professionals who share our passion for clean water and sustainable practices.

2018
Founded in Delhi Started with 3 team members and a dream to purify India
2020
BIS & ISO Certification Achieved all major quality certifications
